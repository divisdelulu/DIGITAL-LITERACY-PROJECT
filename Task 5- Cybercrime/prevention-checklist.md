Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students in India)
Ensure that every account is set with a different strong password (e.g.  Avoid using your name, DOB, or “123456”). 

Turn on 2FA (Two Factor Authentication) on your apps for example Gmail/Instagram/Banking apps.

Never tap on suspicious links received through email, SMS or WhatsApp from unknown sources.

Never log into your bank or any other private account when using public Wi-Fi.

Make sure your device and antivirus patches are up-to-date.

Avoid over-sharing (such as phone number, address, college ID) in on social network sites.

Always check the offers of job/internship before sending documents or the payment of payments.

UPI / Financial Safety Tips:
 Always keep your UPI pin, OTP and bank details to yourself even if they say they are calling from bank.

Always verify that you are transferring money or accepting a “collect request” before entering your UPI PIN.

Reporting Cybercrime:
Report online fraud at: https://cybercrime.gov.in

Call Helpline: 1930 for instant help with financial fraud.
